Alfredo Fettuccine

Description:

a classic Italian pasta dish made with fettuccine, butter, and Parmesan cheese that emulsify to create a rich, creamy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Fettuccine Pasta
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parsley
  • Thyme
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Red Pepper
  • Mozzarilla cheese
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. In a large enough pot, add some water, add some sea salt and butter and put the pot to a boil.
  2. In the boiling water, add the fetttuccine
  3. Dice up the chicken breasts into cubes and season using seasonings of choice.
  4. Dice onions and garlic.
  5. Add a bit of olive oil to a pan and cook the diced chicken breasts thoroghly and put them aside.
  6. In the same pan, saute the garlic then onions.
  7. Add an appropriate amount of heavy cream and stir to prevent anything from sticking to the bottom.
  8. Add some fettuccine cheese and whichever seasonsing.
  9. Add the chicken as well as the fettuccine.
  10. Enjoy!
